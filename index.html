About Us

Noble Plus Processors and Packers Ltd is a company in Kenya providing reliable Contract Manufacturing & Manpower Supply Solutions.

It was founded in the year 2018, by a professional Engineer with the aim of providing full range of services and other range of products in the manufacturing sector to a cross section of clientele at an affordable price.

Mission

To maximize shareholder value through the continuous development and delivery of innovative, top-tier solutions for our Human Resource Operations, consistently surpassing respective industry benchmarks, and fostering a dynamic and challenging work environment for our employees.

Vision

To be the most preferred innovative and consistent manufacturing and human resource supply and general operations management.

Services

👨‍🔧Skilled Manpower Supply

We supply qualified and experienced personnel for industrial and manufacturing environments. Our workforce includes machine operators, production staff, forklift operators, electricians, technicians, supervisors, and maintenance personnel who are trained to work efficiently and professionally.

⌨️Payroll Processing

We offer end-to-end payroll management services designed to ensure employees are paid accurately and on time while maintaining compliance with statutory and regulatory requirements.
