Health and Wellness

Good health is foundational to success. AFBI ensures boys’ well-being through:

✔ Mobile health clinics offering check-ups and health education.
✔ Mental health support, including counseling and peer support groups.
✔ Nutrition programs to foster holistic growth and resilience.
✔ Digital Wellness Programs: Address addiction to gadgets and promote balanced digital consumption.
✔ Buddy Support Systems: Pair boys to support each other emotionally and socially.
